About Research

The Research Club of DIT University takes immense pride in celebrating the birth anniversary of Former President of India, an exceptionally great scientist, Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. We observe the occasion as Anusandhan Diwas on 15th October, dedicating the day towards research and innovations, conducting International Seminar on Innovative Ideas - 2020.
